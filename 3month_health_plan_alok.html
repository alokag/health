💊 Recommended Supplements
Important: Please confirm all supplements with your doctor before starting, especially if you are on any medication. These are evidence-based recommendations based on your lab reports.
🔵 Methylcobalamin (B12)
1000 mcg / day
Your MCV is 101.7 with peripheral macrocytes — clear early B12 insufficiency. As a vegetarian, B12 from diet alone is insufficient.
⏰ Morning, with water, before breakfast
🟡 Omega-3 (Flaxseed / Algal)
1 tbsp ground flaxseed in food daily OR algal oil 250mg DHA
Plant-based omega-3 (ALA from flax, DHA from algae) directly lowers LDL and raises HDL. Fish oil avoided as you are vegetarian.
⏰ With meals (flax in food, oil cap with dinner)
🟢 Vitamin D3
Only if doctor recommends (current level 39.8 is good)
Your level is sufficient (39.8). Maintain with sunlight + food. Supplement only if it drops below 30 on recheck.
⏰ With fatty meal if prescribed (fat-soluble)
🟣 Psyllium Husk (Isabgol)
1 tsp in water before lunch and dinner
Soluble fibre that directly binds LDL cholesterol in the gut before absorption. One of the most proven natural LDL reducers. Meta-analyses show 5–10% LDL reduction.
⏰ 30 min before lunch + dinner with a full glass of water
🩵 Folic Acid (Folate)
Get serum folate tested first
Macrocytosis can be B12 or folate deficiency. Get folate level checked. If low, supplement 400–800 mcg/day alongside B12.
⏰ Morning with B12
🟠 Deworming (one-time)
Albendazole 400 mg — single dose (doctor prescription)
Eosinophils at 11.5% (normal <6%) is very likely due to intestinal parasites. Deworming is safe, effective, and may resolve eosinophilia completely.
⏰ After food (once — doctor to confirm)
